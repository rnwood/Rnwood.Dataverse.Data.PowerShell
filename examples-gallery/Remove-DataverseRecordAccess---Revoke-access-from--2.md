---
title: "Remove-DataverseRecordAccess - Revoke access from a user"
tags: ['Data']
source: "Remove-DataverseRecordAccess.md"
---
Revokes all access from the specified user for the account record. Prompts for confirmation.

```powershell
Remove-DataverseRecordAccess -TableName account -Id "12345678-1234-1234-1234-123456789012" -Principal "87654321-4321-4321-4321-210987654321"

```
