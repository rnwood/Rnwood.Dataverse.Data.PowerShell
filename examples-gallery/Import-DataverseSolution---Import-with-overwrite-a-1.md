tags: ['Solutions']

---
Imports the solution, overwrites unmanaged customizations, publishes workflows, and shows verbose output.

```powershell
Import-DataverseSolution -InFile "C:\Solutions\MySolution.zip" -OverwriteUnmanagedCustomizations -PublishWorkflows -Verbose

```
