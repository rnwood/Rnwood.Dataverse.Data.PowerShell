---
title: "Remove-DataverseView - Remove a system view"
tags: ['Metadata']
source: "Remove-DataverseView.md"
---
Removes a system view by its ID. ViewType must be specified when deleting system views.

```powershell
Remove-DataverseView -Id "12345678-1234-1234-1234-123456789012" -ViewType "System"

```
