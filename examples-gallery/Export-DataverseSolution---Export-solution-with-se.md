title: "Export-DataverseSolution - Export solution with settings included"
tags: ['Solutions']
source: "Export-DataverseSolution.md"
---
Exports "MySolution" including auto-numbering and calendar settings.

```powershell
Export-DataverseSolution -SolutionName "MySolution" -ExportAutoNumberingSettings -ExportCalendarSettings -OutFile "C:\Exports\MySolution.zip"

```
