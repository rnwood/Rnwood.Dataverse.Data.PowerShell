tags: ['Metadata']

---
Retrieves a specific sitemap by its unique name.

```powershell
Get-DataverseSitemap -UniqueName "mysitemap_unique"

# Name              Id                                   UniqueName
# ----             --                                  ----------
# MySitemap         a1b2c3d4-5678-90ab-cdef-1234567890ab mysitemap_unique

```
