title: "Remove-DataverseFormControl - Remove control with confirmation"
tags: ['Metadata']
source: "Remove-DataverseFormControl.md"
---
Removes the control with user confirmation prompt before proceeding.

```powershell
Remove-DataverseFormControl -FormId $formId -TabName 'General' -SectionName 'MainInfo' -DataField 'telephone2' -Confirm

```
