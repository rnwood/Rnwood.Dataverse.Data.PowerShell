---
title: "Remove-DataverseAppModule - Remove an app module by ID"
tags: ['Metadata']
source: "Remove-DataverseAppModule.md"
---
Removes the app module with the specified ID without confirmation.

```powershell
Remove-DataverseAppModule -Id "12345678-1234-1234-1234-123456789012" -Confirm:$false

```
