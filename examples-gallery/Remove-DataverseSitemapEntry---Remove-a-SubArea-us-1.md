title: "Remove-DataverseSitemapEntry - Remove a SubArea using pipeline"
tags: ['Metadata']

---
Retrieves a specific SubArea entry and removes it via pipeline.

```powershell
Get-DataverseSitemapEntry -SitemapName "Default" -EntryType SubArea -EntryId "Contacts" | Remove-DataverseSitemapEntry

```
