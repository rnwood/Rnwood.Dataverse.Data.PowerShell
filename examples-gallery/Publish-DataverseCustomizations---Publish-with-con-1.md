---
title: "Publish-DataverseCustomizations - Publish with confirmation"
tags: ['Solutions']
source: "Publish-DataverseCustomizations.md"
---
Publishes customizations with explicit confirmation.

```powershell
Publish-DataverseCustomizations -Confirm

# Confirm
# Are you sure you want to perform this action?
# Performing the operation "Publish" on target "All customizations".
# [Y] Yes  [A] Yes to All  [N] No  [L] No to All  [S] Suspend  [?] Help (default is "Y"): Y
# Customizations published successfully.

```
