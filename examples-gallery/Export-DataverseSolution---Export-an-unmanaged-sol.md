---
title: "Export-DataverseSolution - Export an unmanaged solution to a file"
tags: ['Solutions']
source: "Export-DataverseSolution.md"
---
Exports the unmanaged version of "MySolution" and saves it to the specified file.

```powershell
Export-DataverseSolution -SolutionName "MySolution" -OutFile "C:\Exports\MySolution.zip"

```
