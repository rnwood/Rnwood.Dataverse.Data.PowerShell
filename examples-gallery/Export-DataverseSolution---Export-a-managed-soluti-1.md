title: "Export-DataverseSolution - Export a managed solution with progress monitoring"
tags: ['Solutions']

---
Exports the managed version of "MySolution" with verbose output showing the export progress.

```powershell
Export-DataverseSolution -SolutionName "MySolution" -Managed -OutFile "C:\Exports\MySolution_managed.zip" -Verbose

```
