---
title: "Remove-DataverseRelationshipMetadata - Delete a ManyToMany relationship without confirmation"
tags: ['Metadata']
source: "Remove-DataverseRelationshipMetadata.md"
---
Deletes a ManyToMany relationship without prompting. All association records are deleted.

```powershell
Remove-DataverseRelationshipMetadata -SchemaName new_project_contact_association -Force

```
