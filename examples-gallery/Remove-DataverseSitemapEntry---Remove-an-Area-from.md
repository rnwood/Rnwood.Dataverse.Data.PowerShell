---
title: "Remove-DataverseSitemapEntry - Remove an Area from a sitemap"
tags: ['Metadata']
source: "Remove-DataverseSitemapEntry.md"
---
Removes the Area with ID "Sales" from the sitemap named "Default".

```powershell
Remove-DataverseSitemapEntry -SitemapName "Default" -EntryType Area -EntryId "Sales"

```
